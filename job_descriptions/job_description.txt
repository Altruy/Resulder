About the job

Working alongside a talented team of researchers, engineers, and domain experts, you'll tackle challenging problems and deliver innovative solutions that make a real impact. Requirements

Key Responsibilities:

 Algorithm Development: Design, implement, and optimize AI algorithms and models to solve complex problems across diverse domains, including but not limited to natural language processing, computer vision, and reinforcement learning
 Model Training and Evaluation: Train machine learning models using large-scale datasets, evaluating model performance and iteratively refining approaches to achieve desired outcomes
 Model Deployment: Architect scalable and reliable AI pipelines for deployment in production environments, collaborating with DevOps engineers to ensure seamless integration and monitoring
 Data Processing: Develop data pipelines to ingest, clean, and preprocess data for use in AI systems, ensuring data quality and integrity throughout the data lifecycle
 Performance Optimization: Optimize AI models and systems for speed, efficiency, and scalability, utilizing techniques such as parallel processing and distributed computing
 Cross-Functional Collaboration: Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including data scientists, software engineers, and product managers, to define requirements, prioritize tasks, and deliver solutions that meet business objectives
 Documentation and Reporting: Prepare documentation and technical reports to communicate algorithm designs, implementation details, and performance metrics to stakeholders, ensuring transparency and reproducibility


Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Statistics, or related field
Strong background in artificial intelligence, with hands-on experience in developing and deploying AI algorithms and models in production environments
Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, or scikit-learn, with experience in data manipulation and analysis
Familiarity with AI concepts and techniques, including supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, and reinforcement learning
Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud, and proficiency in using relevant tools and services for AI development and deployment
Strong problem-solving abilities and analytical thinking, with a keen attention to detail and a passion for tackling complex technical challenges
Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams and communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
